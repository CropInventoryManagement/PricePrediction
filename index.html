Price Prediction

Agriculture is the art and science of cultivating the soil, growing crops and raising livestock.

This page is designed to forecast the future prices of various agricultural products. We use machine learning to appropriately predict the volume of the product to dispatch at a given point of time to ensure proper flow of goods and to avoid pilling up of produce in the storage house.

Price prediction uses an algorithm to analyze a product or service based on its characteristics, demand and current market trends.

Here we have used time series learning algorithm to forecast the price. This will help us to manage the inventory system efficiently.
